Integrity Infrastructure

AI-proctored assessments for open courses at national scale.

Classagon’s proctoring layer is designed to keep open learning outcomes dependable for institutions, employers, and ambitious learners.

How Proctoring Works

01. Session Integrity Signals

Participation consistency, interaction anomalies, and focus events are monitored continuously in assessment windows.

02. Flag + Review Workflow

AI flags are routed to human moderation queues to reduce false positives and preserve fairness across learner cohorts.

03. Audit Trails

Timestamped records support dispute resolution, institutional quality reviews, and compliance evidence when needed.

Proctoring Philosophy

Access + Trust

Assistive, Not Punitive

Signals guide risk scoring and review, not default penalties.

Institution-Ready
