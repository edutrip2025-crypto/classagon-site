How the proctoring layer works

The goal is not to police learning. The goal is to make the assessment outcome defensible when it matters.

Step 01

Session integrity signals

Classagon watches for presence consistency, attention shifts, interruption patterns, and environment anomalies during protected assessment windows.

Step 02

Flag and review workflow

AI does not issue verdicts in isolation. Risk events are routed into review queues so institutions can apply judgment with context and fairness.

Step 03

Audit trails that survive scrutiny

Each critical event is time-linked and reviewable, supporting moderation, dispute handling, and downstream confidence in the final certificate.

Proctoring philosophy

This layer is designed to help open learning earn institutional trust without making the learner experience punitive.

Access + trust

Open enrollment should not force low-confidence credentials. Accessibility and credibility need to coexist in the same learning model.

Assistive, not punitive

Signals help identify risk and guide review. The system is built to create evidence, not perform arbitrary punishment.

Institution-ready from day one

Schools, workforce academies, and training organizations need process integrity they can explain. Classagon gives them that language and evidence.
